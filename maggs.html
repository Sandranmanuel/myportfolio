Project information

  • Location: Ennis, Ireland

  • Technologies: Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere

Mna Ag Gaire is an enterprise of committed and passionate women who organize activities and events to support the Ennis Women's Shed.

The project intends to design promotional graphic and web assets such as Posters, Flyers, Booklets, promotional videos, etc., maintaining their ethos and brand identity.
