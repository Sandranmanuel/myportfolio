Carina Silva - Branding

Project information

  • Category: Graphic Design
  • Client: Carina Silva
  • Location: Bantry, Cork, Ireland
  • Project date: September 2021
  • Technologies: Adobe Illustrator

Carina has a partnership with Life and Health Coaching that aims to unlock the inner potential of the individual, using honest and direct conversation, encouraging, and supporting clients to take accountability for their goals.

This project intends to develop a brand identity, identify colours, design a logo and a business card that best describes their action.
